Claude Bajada

Claude Bajada is a lecturer at the Department of Physiology and Biochemistry at the University of Malta with a particular interest in the confluence of neural architecture, brain function and MRI techniques. His PhD work, at the University of Manchester, focused on using novel approaches to elucidate the white matter architecture of the temporal lobe. Claude currently leads the Large-scale data collection working group for COST Action CA18106.
