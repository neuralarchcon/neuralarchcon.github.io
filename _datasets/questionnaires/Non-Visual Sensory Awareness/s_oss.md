A self-report questionnaire designed to measure individual significance and sensitivity to olfactory stimuli. The scale assesses how important smell is in daily life and how sensitive individuals are to different odors.

#### Detailed description

The OSS measures subjective olfactory sensitivity and the role of olfaction in everyday experiences. This questionnaire helps characterize individual differences in how people perceive and respond to smells in their environment.
