#### Detailed description

The EQ-Short includes 22 items that refer to an ability to recognize thoughts or feelings of another person and to react to these thoughts and feelings with adequate behavior.

The scale allows for assessment of empathy as a cognitive and affective ability to understand and respond to others' mental and emotional states.
