Datasets

The NeurArchCon database is a multi-site investigation of individual differences in consciousness, collected across Krakow, Aarhus, and Brno. The database adopts a multidimensional approach, investigating key dimensions through behavioral tasks, questionnaires, and neurobiological measurements: visual awareness, non-visual sensory awareness, memory, metacognition, integration, emotional state, personality, states of consciousness, and intelligence.

Behavioral Component

20+ experimental tasks measuring distinct aspects of consciousness through detection and discrimination paradigms, bistable perception tasks (binocular rivalry, structure from motion, Necker cube), metacognitive tasks with confidence ratings, and multisensory integration paradigms.

Questionnaire Battery

Validated instruments including VVIQ-2 (visual imagery), MAIA-2 (interoceptive awareness), NEO-PI-R (personality), and measures of memory function, emotional states, mindfulness, and states of consciousness.

Neurobiological Data

MRI: Resting-state fMRI, Multiparameter Mapping (MPM), diffusion-weighted imaging (DWI). EEG: High-density recordings during standard and naturalistic states. Genetics: Raw genetic data available for analysis. All data are BIDS-compliant with rigorous quality control.
